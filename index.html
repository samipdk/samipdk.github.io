IPEDS Enrollment & Outcome Intelligence — Midwest Universities

90 public universities · 6 states · 2019–2024

Panel study of enrollment trends, retention rates, and 6-year graduation outcomes across 90 Midwest public 4-year universities. ETL pipeline merging 18 IPEDS CSVs on UNITID into SQLite for structured SQL EDA across 6 business questions. Key finding: Iowa averages 72.6% graduation rate — 37.5 points above Oklahoma. Every top-10 institution retains 85%+ of students. Retention is the single strongest predictor of 6-year graduation success.
